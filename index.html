Welcome to Dev Bhoomi Uttarakhand University Dehradun

Dev Bhoomi Uttarakhand University (DBUU), founded in 2005 as Dev Bhoomi Institute of Technology, has since evolved into a distinguished non-profit autonomous university, recognized by the UGC under Section 2(f) of the UGC Act, 1956 Dev Bhoomi Uttarakhand University collegedehradun.com . Spanning a lush 42-acre campus in Dehradun, DBUU offers over 120 programs across 11 schools, including areas like Engineering, Management, Law, Design, Agriculture, Hospital Administration, Mass Communication, Nursing, and Pharmacy Dev Bhoomi Group of Institutions (DBGI) first opened its gates in 2005 intending to provide excellent quality education, combining world-class infrastructure, qualified faculty, and a diverse student community.

Awarded for Best Placement College in Dehradun, Uttarakhand By MHRD

Being the Best Engineering college in Dehradun encompasses a blend of a vibrant array of cultures, opinions, and unparalleled prospects, preparing students for future leadership roles.
